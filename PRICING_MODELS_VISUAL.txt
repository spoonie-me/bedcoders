KEY TAKEAWAYS
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Current: High friction (€199 or nothing) → 3% conversion

   Hybrid: Captures 60% with lower entry barrier

4. Codecademy's genius: Multiple entry points (€0, €14.99, €35, €300)
   Medinformics hybrid: Multiple entry points (€0, €49, €29, €299, €199)

5. You win on: Lifetime access + AI feedback + Certifications
   You lose on: Complexity (4 tiers vs. Codecademy's 3)

6. Solution: Simplify tiers (drop "Two Tracks"), clarify messaging
   Then: +20–35% revenue without changing pedagogy
